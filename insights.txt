Insights from Sales Dataset

1. West Region Leads Sales:
The dataset shows strong dominance of sales from western states like California, indicating the West region contributes the highest share of overall revenue.

2. Office Supplies is the Most Purchased Category:
Office Supplies has the highest frequency of purchases, reflecting regular, high-volume demand from customers compared to other categories.

3. Presence of High-Value Enterprise Orders:
The maximum single sale recorded is ₹22,638.48, showing that large enterprise-level purchases significantly influence revenue distribution.

4. Majority of Orders Are Low to Mid-Value:
50% of transactions fall below ₹54.49, suggesting that typical customer orders are small to medium-sized, though occasional big-ticket items raise the average to ₹230.76.
